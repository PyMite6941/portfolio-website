Description


A React flashcard app for learning vocabulary in a foreign language. Cards show a word or phrase in the target language and flip to reveal the translation. You can mark cards as known or unknown to track your progress, and the deck reshuffles so you keep drilling the ones you missed. Built with React and Vite, deployed on Vercel.
